Our Story

Because there's no way to improve without looking back on where you came from.



2013

aThemes started back in 2013 with releasing free WordPress
themes on WordPress.org. After some early success with
themes like Fashionista and Hiero, we decided to keep
releasing themes and see where it would take us.



2014

In August 2014 we released our first premium theme, Alizee
Pro. This was received well enough for us to keep creating
and releasing easy to use themes, both free and premium.



2018

As of 2018, our Sydney theme has reached an incredible
milestone: with 200,000 active installs, it’s one of the most
popular free WordPress themes in the world. Its premium
version, Sydney Pro, now comes with four additional demos
you can import, giving you five designs for the price of one.



2019

The Airi theme was a new addition to our free themes. It
reached 10,000 installs within the year, and we added the
premium Airi Pro companion plugin. We also gave our
Sydney theme a design refresh and continued to release
new designs for Sydney Pro.



2020

We keep on improving our existing themes to make them
even more awesome. We are working hard behind the
themes to make more layouts for popular themes like
Sydney Pro and Airi Pro.
